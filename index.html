About Us

We have been in the health care industry for more than 10 years, cumulatively. We have experience in patient care, both front and behind the scenes. One of our members is a US Navy veteran who dedicated part of his life to serving the country. For all these reasons, we are committed to providing excellent, quality service throughout the entire Las Vegas Valley. We own and operate the business, guaranteeing that we will care for you or your loved one like family.

Safety is our top priority at our company. We have all been fully vaccinated against COVID-19 since 2021 and have received BLS certification from the American Heart Association. We all have received proper training in driving and proper securement of passengers. In addition, our technicians wear masks and gloves for extra precaution. We also ensure the cleanliness of our vehicle, which is disinfected at the beginning of the day and after every trip, to provide a safe and secure transportation experience.

Mission Statement

Reliance Care Enterprise LLC will provide safe, comfortable, and stress-free non-emergency medical transportation to and from any medical facility, caring for you like family in transit.

What We Do

We have been certified by the Nevada Transportation Authority to transport ambulatory, wheelchair-user, and bed-bound individuals to and/or from the following:

  • Medical Appointments
  • Dialysis Centers
  • Hospital Discharges
  • Skilled Nursing Facilities
  • Assisted Living Facilities
  • Adult Day Services
  • Behavioral Health
  • Dental Appointments
  • Doctors Appointments
  • Dialysis Transportation
  • Hospice Care
  • Hospital Discharge
  • Hospital Admissions
  • Independent Living Facilities
  • Inter-Facility Transfers
  • Mental Health Treatment
  • Nursing Homes
  • Optometrist Visits
  • Physical Therapy
  • Para-Transit Services
  • Rehabilitation Appointments
  • Retirement Homes
  • Senior Centers
  • Special Needs Transports

We will transport you in our clean, spacious, well-maintained, and comfortable passenger van custom-equipped with a wheelchair lift and proper tie-down equipment for a secure, safe, comfortable, and stress-free commute to your destination in a timely manner.

Our Services

Wheelchair Transport

Our vehicle is equipped with a Braunability wheelchair lift to allow easy entry and comfort for exceptional transportation service. Our trained, friendly, and helpful staff will assist every wheelchair user to ensure they receive a stress-free and enjoyable ride.

Gurney Transport

For passengers needing gurney transportation, we offer a meticulous bed- to-bed service to ensure that safety and care are met every step of the way. Our staff has undergone extensive hands-on training to provide the passenger with a safe, smooth, and comfortable transition.

Door-To-Door

Our door-to-door approach to transporting people sets our service apart from others. We pick up our customers from their front door. We can even help transfer a person to their wheelchair from their bed or chair. True to our slogan, we firmly believe that taking this extra step will create a more enjoyable and comfortable ride; entrusting themselves and their loved ones to us produces peace of mind.
